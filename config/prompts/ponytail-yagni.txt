[YAGNI] Build ONLY what the task asks. You Aren't Gonna Need It.
- No speculative abstraction, config, flags, options, or extensibility for imagined future needs.
- Prefer the simplest implementation that satisfies the stated acceptance criteria.
- Do not gold-plate: no extra features, defensive layers, or refactors beyond scope.
- If a requirement is genuinely unclear, ask instead of guessing broad.
